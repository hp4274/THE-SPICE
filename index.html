About Us

The Spice was created to bring the authentic taste of India to Blackpool. We believe food brings people together, so our chefs prepare traditional Indian dishes with fresh ingredients, aromatic herbs, and authentic spices that celebrate real flavour in every plate.

Located inside The Strand Hotel, our restaurant offers a relaxed atmosphere with beautiful sea views where families and friends can enjoy quality food together. From the buffet counter to the dessert selection, every detail is designed for easy dining and warm hospitality.

Menu

Accompaniments

1. Salad

A fresh, crisp mix of garden greens, sliced cucumber, cherry tomatoes, and red onions, lightly dressed to cleanse the palate.

2. Papadums

Crisp, sun-dried lentil wafers flash-fried until light and crackly. Served as the perfect crunchy appetite-starter.

3. Mint Yogurt

A cool, refreshing yogurt blend whisked with fresh mint leaves, grated cucumber, and a touch of roasted cumin to perfectly balance spicy flavors.

4. Red Chutney

A vibrant, tangy-sweet condiment made from crisp red bell peppers, cucumber, and onions, blended with rich tomato ketchup and a kick of fiery red chili paste.

Veg Starters

1. Chilli Paneer

Crispy paneer cubes tossed in a wok with crunchy peppers, onions, and spicy soy-chili glaze.

2. Veg Samosa

Crisp, golden pastry pyramids stuffed with a spiced mixture of smashed potatoes, green peas, and fragrant cumin. Served with tangy chutneys.

3. Hara Bhara Kebab

Pan-seared spinach, green pea, and potato patties, subtly spiced and enriched with fresh coriander and mint. Crispy on the outside, soft on the inside.

4. Crispy Bhaji

Lightly battered, deeply fried onion and vegetable fritters, seasoned with traditional spices for the ultimate crunch.

Non-Veg Starters

1. Chicken Tikka

Succulent boneless chicken marinated overnight in yogurt, aromatic spices, and herbs, then roasted in the tandoor to achieve a smoky char and juicy finish.

2. Chilli Chicken

Crispy chicken pieces tossed in a bold Indo-Chinese sauce with fresh peppers, onions, garlic, and green chillies, delivering the perfect balance of spice and savoury flavours.

3. Lamb Seekh Kebab

Minced lamb blended with fresh herbs, fragrant spices, and onions, hand-shaped onto skewers and grilled in the tandoor until tender, smoky, and full of flavour.

4. Meat Samosa

Golden, flaky pastry filled with a savoury mixture of seasoned minced meat, onions, and traditional spices, fried until perfectly crisp and served as a classic starter.

Veg Mains

1. Chana Masala

Tender chickpeas slow-simmered in a rustic, tangier onion-tomato gravy infused with a traditional spice blend, fresh ginger, and green chilies.

2. Saag Aloo

Tender, diced potatoes cooked with fresh, iron-rich spinach and a blend of aromatic spices, garlic, and ginger.

3. Paneer Butter Masala

Golden cubes of fresh cottage cheese simmered in a rich, velvety tomato and cashew nut gravy, finished with a touch of butter, cream, and dried fenugreek leaves.

4. Tadka Dal

Nutritious yellow lentils slow-simmered with turmeric, garlic, and ginger, finished with a fresh, aromatic tempering (tadka) of ghee, cumin seeds, and smoky whole red chilies.

Non-Veg Mains

1. Chicken Tikka Masala

Tender, clay-oven-roasted chicken pieces simmered in a rich, creamy tomato gravy. A crowd-favourite masterpiece.

2. Chicken Korma

Succulent chicken slow-cooked in a velvety, mild sauce of yogurt, cream, and delicate cashew paste. Pure comfort.

3. Chicken Karahi

A robust, wok-tossed classic featuring diced chicken, fresh tomatoes, ginger, and crushed spices. Vibrant and full of character.

4. Lamb Maharaja

Regal, tender chunks of lamb slow-braised in a prestigious, deeply layered gravy infused with exotic spices. Fit for royalty.

Bread & Rice

Garlic and Coriander Naan

Freshly baked, pillow-soft flatbread evenly dusted with savory garlic powder and fresh coriander, charred beautifully in our clay oven.

Jeera Rice

Fragrant, fluffy basmati rice delicately tossed with toasted cumin seeds.

Desserts & Ice Cream

Gulab Jamun with Ice Cream

Warm, golden-fried milk dumplings soaked in a fragrant cardamom and rosewater syrup, served alongside a cool scoop of rich vanilla ice cream for the ultimate hot-and-cold contrast.

Chocolate Ice Cream

A rich, velvety scoop of deeply indulgent premium cocoa ice cream, crafted for true chocolate lovers.

Strawberry Ice Cream

A refreshing, creamy scoop infused with the vibrant, sweet flavor of ripe, sun-ripened strawberries.

Vanilla Ice Cream

A timeless, smooth classic made with real vanilla bean extract for a rich, aromatic, and comforting flavor.
